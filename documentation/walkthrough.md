## 🚀 1. The Onboarding Experience

When you first launch Neon Hub, you are greeted by the **Home / Discover** page.
- At the top right, click **Login / Register**.
- Create a new account with your email.
- Upon successful registration, you are instantly logged in and redirected to your **Dashboard**.

> [!TIP]
> Your dashboard will initially be empty. It's time to add some media!

## 🔍 2. Finding Media

Use the top navigation bar to search for your favorite content.
1. Click the **Search Icon**.
2. Type a query (e.g., `"The Last of Us"`).
3. The real-time debounced search will query TMDB (for movies/TV) and RAWG (for games) simultaneously.
4. Click on a search result to view its detail page.

## 📥 3. Building Your Library

On any media detail page (Movie, Series, or Game), you will see an **"+ Add to Library"** button.
1. Click it to add the item to your library.
2. Once added, the button transforms into a status selector. Click it to update the status (e.g., from *Planned* to *Completed*).
3. Click the **Heart Icon** to mark the item as a Favorite.
4. You can also log **Hours Played** for games directly from the detail page.

## ✍️ 4. Reviewing and Rating

After finishing a game or movie, share your thoughts:
1. Click the **Write Review** button on the detail page.
2. A sleek modal will appear. Select a rating out of 10 and write your text review.
3. Your review is immediately posted and visible under the Reviews section of that item.

## 🤖 5. The AI Experience (Gemini)

Neon Hub uses AI to supercharge your library.

### Generating Recommendations
1. Navigate to your **Library**.
2. Click the shiny **Recommend with AI** button.
3. The system sends your current tracked library to Google Gemini, which analyzes your tastes and generates 5 highly personalized recommendations, complete with reasons *why* you will like them based on your existing library.

### AI Comparisons
1. In your Library, toggle the **Compare** mode.
2. Select exactly two items (e.g., *Interstellar* and *The Martian*).
3. Click **Compare with AI**.
4. You will be taken to a dedicated, side-by-side comparison page where Gemini breaks down the story, visuals, strengths, and weaknesses of both items, ending with a final verdict.

## 📊 6. Checking Your Stats

Return to the **Dashboard** via the navigation bar.
- See your **Total Tracked** items jump up.
- Watch your **Progress Rings** fill as you complete media.
- Review your dynamic **Genre Chart** to see what types of media you consume the most.
- Check the **Activity Feed** to see a chronological log of all the actions you've taken.

## 🌐 7. Language and Localization

Want to experience the app in Arabic?
1. Click the Language Selector in the top right corner.
2. Select **العربية**.
3. Watch the entire application seamlessly transition to a RTL (Right-to-Left) layout, with fully translated strings and components!
